# European VC — capital deployed by country (2024)

Reference figures for venture-capital investment into European startups by
country of headquarters, full-year 2024. Numbers are reported approximate
US-dollar totals and deal counts rounded to the nearest plausible value.

**Source:** Atomico — *State of European Tech 2024*; Dealroom year-end
European venture review 2024.
