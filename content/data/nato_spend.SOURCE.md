# European NATO defence spend (2024)

Reference figures for European NATO members' defence expenditure in 2024
in US dollars billion and as a share of GDP. Rendered alongside the
defense-tech venture data to give the macro picture.

**Source:** NATO — *Defence Expenditure of NATO Countries (2014–2024)*.
